Slow Cooked Ribs

Description

This tasty slow cooked recipe will create tender, fall of the bone meat with a slightly-sweet kick

Ingredients

  • One rack of ribs
  • Brown sugar: 2 Tablespoons
  • Garlic Powder: 1 Tablespoon
  • Onion Powder: 1 Tablespoon
  • Paprika: 1 Tablespoon
  • Chili Powder: 1 Tablespoon
  • Cayenne Pepper: 1 Teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 Teaspoon
  • Pepper: 1 Teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 275 degrees
  2. Mix all seasonings together in a bowl
  3. Using a paper towel or equivalent, dry off ribs of any excess moisture and remove excess skin from backside of ribs
  4. Rub the mixed seasonings over and into the ribs, being liberal with the portion
  5. Place seasoned ribs on a baking pan, using wax paper if desired
  6. Place ribs into heated oven for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the meat is fully tender
