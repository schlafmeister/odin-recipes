Duck Fried Rice

This recipe will allow you to make the most of either leftovers or freshly prepared duck and rice. It is quick and easy and will leave your mouth watering after minimal effort with its flavor and richness!

Ingredients

  • Duck: One Pound cooked and shredded
  • Sliced mushrooms: One cup
  • Coconut Aminos: half cup or enough to flavor without soaking
  • Rice: One cup, already cooked

Instructions

  1. Begin sauteeing mushrooms with a bit of garlic and salt
  2. Mix in rice and duck and aminos, allow all to get hot and carmelized
  3. Stir and wait until there is slight char and carmelization within the bulk of food.
